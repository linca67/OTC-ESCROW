How it works
STEP 01

Create & deposit

Party A locks their tokens and sets what they want in return. Share the trade ID with your counterparty.

STEP 02

Fill the trade

Party B deposits their side. Funds from both parties are now locked in the contract — safe from either party.

STEP 03

Both confirm → swap

Once both parties confirm, the atomic swap executes. Each party receives what they agreed. No intermediary ever touches your funds.
